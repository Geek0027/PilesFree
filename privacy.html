PilesFree Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2023

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At PilesFree, we are committed to protecting your personal information while providing our Ayurvedic piles treatment services. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our products and services.

Information We Collect

When you order PilesFree, we may collect:

  • Personal Details: Name, contact information, shipping address
  • Order Information: Products purchased, payment method (though we don't store full payment details)
  • Health Information: Only what you voluntarily share about your piles condition
  • Website Usage Data: How you interact with our website (through cookies)

How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively for:

  • Processing and delivering your PilesFree orders
  • Providing customer support for our Ayurvedic products
  • Improving our piles treatment formulations and services
  • Sending important updates about your order (never spam)

Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure servers with encryption for all stored data
  • Limited access to only essential personnel
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Never selling or sharing your data with third parties for marketing

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data (unless we're required to keep it by law)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@pilesfree.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted here with the updated date.
